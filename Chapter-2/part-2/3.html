(Q3).A = {set of quadrilaterals}, B = {square, rectangle, trapezium, rhombus}. State whether A ⊂ B or B ⊂ A. Justify your answer.

A = {set of quadrilaterals} means A = {square, rectangle/trapezium, rhombus, parallelogram}

B = {square, rectangle, trapezium, rhombus}

So, B ⊂ A because A’ is having elements than ‘B’.
