(Q1).Identify and write the “common property” of the following collections.

(1) 2, 4, 6, 8, …….

Answer: All are numbers

(2) 2, 3, 5, 7, 11, …….

Answer: All are numbers

(3) 1, 4, 9, 16, …….

Answer: All are squares

(4) January, February, March, April,…

Answer: All are English

(5) Thumb, index finger, middle finger, ring finger, pinky.

Answer: All are of a hand
