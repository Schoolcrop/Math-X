(Q15).Logarithmic form of 100 = 10z .

What is Logarithmic form?

The logarithmic form is written as log a(c)=b. it is a rearrangement of the exponential form, ab=c Any exponential equation can be written as a logarithm.

100 = 10z

b = 100

a = 10

c = z

Here a,b,c can be written as per the given formula

log ab = c (formula)

log 100 = z

Log form log 100 = z

So it is Log 100 = z
