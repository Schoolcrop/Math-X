(Q3).Consider the numbers of the form 4n where n is a natural number. Check whether there is any value of n for which 4n ends with zero?

If 4n is to end with zero for a natural number n, it should be divisible by 2 and 5

Prime factors of 4n should contain 2 and 5

4n = 22n

Here only 2 is factor.

Hence no natural number n for which 4n ends with the digit .
