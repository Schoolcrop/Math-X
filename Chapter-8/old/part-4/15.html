(Q15) ABC is an isosceles triangle right angled at C. Prove that AB2 = 2AC2.

Solution :

Given : In ∆ABC; ∠C = 90° AC = BC.

R.T.P : 2 = 2

Proof : In ∆ACB; ∠C = 90°

Hence, AC2 + BC2 = AB2

[Square of the hypotenuse is equal to sum of the squares of the other two sides - Pythagoras theorem]

⇒ AC2 + ">2 = AB2 [∵ AC = BC given]

⇒ = 2
