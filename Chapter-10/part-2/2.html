(Q2).A sphere is inscribed in a cylinder. Is the surface of the sphere equal to the curved surface of the cylinder? If yes, explain how.

Answer:

, the surface area of the sphere is to the curved surface area of the cylinder.

Let the radius of the “cylinder be ‘r’ and its height ‘h’.

Then radius of sphere = r.

Then curved surface area of cylinder = Then curved surface area of cylinder = πrh = 2πr ( + r)

[∵ height = diameter of the sphere = diameter of the cylinder = 2r]

= 2πr (r) = πr2

And surface area of the sphere = πr2

∴ C.S.A. of cylinder = Surface area of sphere.
